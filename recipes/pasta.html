Ingredients

      Pasta What I love about this garlic chicken pasta recipe is you can literally use any kind of pasta that you like. I typically alter my recipe, depending on what kind of pasta I found on sale.
      olive oil You want a good quality olive oil to cook the chicken in.
      Tomatoes The tomatoes enhance the way the chicken pasta looks, but it also makes it
      Butter The butter helps cook the garlic and adds to the flavor.
      Garlic For best results, use minced garlic. I stick around to four cloves for this recipe, but you could always add in more or less depending on what you like.
      Heavy cream The real star of the show is the heavy cream! It gives this chicken pasta a creamy texture that is crave-worthy.
      Spinach Spinach gives you a boost in vitamins and minerals. You can leave it out if you want to, but I love the flavor.
      Parmesan cheese You can't really have an Italian dish without some Parmesan cheese, right?
      Chicken I typically use boneless skinless chicken breast; you could also use boneless skinless chicken thighs. It's entirely up to you, and whatever you prefer will be fine.

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt to the boiling water. This will help season the pasta as it cooks.
  2. Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the package until it reaches your desired level of tenderness. Drain the pasta once it's cooked.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the other ingredients. Chop the tomatoes, mince the garlic, and slice the chicken into small, bite-sized pieces.
  4. In a separate pan, heat olive oil and butter over medium heat.
  5. Add minced garlic to the pan and saut√© until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  6. Add the chicken to the pan and cook until it's no longer pink in the center. Make sure it's cooked through.
  7. Add the chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook for a few minutes until they soften and release their juices.
  8. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes, allowing it to thicken slightly.
  9. Stir in the spinach and cook until wilted.
  10. Add the cooked and drained pasta to the sauce. Toss everything together to ensure the pasta is well coated with the sauce.
  11. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and toss again to combine all the ingredients.
  12. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
